Chicken Carbonara

Description

This chicken carbonara pasta bake is the perfect Sunday dinner choice for the whole family. Serve with a side of roasted vegetables, a salad, and some crusty bread to soak up extra sauce.

Ingredients

  • Pancetta
  • 16 ounces os spaghetti
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 2 cups of heavy cream
  • Chicken breast
  • 2 cups of parmesan cheese
  • Parsley optional

How to cook the Chicken

  1. Heat skillet over medium heat
  2. Add a tablespoon of oil and the chicken breast
  3. cook for about 16 minutes, flipping half way, until an instant read thermometer registers 170 degree F

Steps to make the Chicken Carbonara

  1. Cook the pachetta until lightly browned and crisp, then set aside
  2. Boil spaghetti, reserve two cups of pasta water and set aside
  3. Whisk eggs in a bowl, then whisk in 1/2 cups of hot pasta water
  4. Whisk the cream and cheese
  5. Add the pasta, pachette and chicken to the sauce and toss
  6. transfer the mixture to the baking dish until the sauce is thickened
  7. Stir and let stand to a prepared baking dish intil the sauce is thickened
  8. Garnish with parsley and Parmesan cheese
